About Integration Bee

The Integration Bee is a mathematics competition testing participants' proficiency in solving integrals accurately and efficiently. It challenges both speed and precision under timed conditions.

Ideal for those who enjoy thinking quickly and solving problems creatively. This is where calculus enthusiasts battle it out to prove their mastery over integration techniques!

Preliminary Round

Format: Written Test
Problems: 15 Integrals
Time: 60 Minutes
Difficulty: Varying Levels

Advancement

Top scorers from the preliminary round will qualify for the Final Round. This round tests your fundamental integration skills across various techniques and difficulty levels.

Final Round

Knockout Tournament on Board
Format: Head-to-Head Battles
Time per Problem: 3-5 Minutes
Structure: One vs One Knockout
Winner: Top 2 Participants

How It Works

  • Each round: one integral per two participants
  • First to solve correctly advances; the other is eliminated
  • Partial marks awarded if both participants cannot solve
  • Sudden-death tiebreaker if needed
  • Solutions presented on board for all to see
